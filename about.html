About



Mission


❝WE AIM TO BRING TO OUR CUSTOMERS TAILORED STRATEGIES SO THAT THEY CAN ACHIEVE THEIR GOALS SUSTAINABLY AND WITH THE FINEST RESULTS❞


We started our project on the base of a solid objective: to bridge East and West and support our clients to expand their horizons both geographically and in terms of growth. With our multicultural roots, we aim to provide high-quality services we can be proud of.




Vision



Our vision is to become the connectors and enablers of our client's dreams. We want them to feel empowered to aim big, knowing that they have strong professionals to support their steps into new lands and sectors. We love helping brands and companies into new markets and to bridge them culturally and structurally to succeed sustainably.




Our Values



We work following our values: environmental and social sustainability, respect and support of minorities, support to the development of women entrepreneurship, the aim to achieve gender and diversity balance in business and society.






Our story

Marta and Rossella started the business in 2020 to follow the need of creating their own company based on their experience and values. They follow intuition and passion aiming to be a bridge between East and West, Europe, and Asia. Our co-founders put their whole expertise and enthusiasm into every project and want to progress with their clients with professionalism and deep understanding.




Providing a top-class quality to our partners and clients
